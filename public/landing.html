How it works

Three steps.
One breakthrough.

01 · FETCH

Drop a URL

Our Python fetcher pulls the full transcript from any public YouTube video. Captions, auto-generated or manual — we grab them all instantly.

02 · PROCESS

LLaMA reads it

Your question + the full transcript is sent to LLaMA 3.1-70B via Groq. Multi-key rotation means zero rate-limit delays at scale.

03 · LEARN

Ask anything

Summaries, quizzes, timestamp references — every answer is grounded in the exact words of the video. No hallucinations. No fluff.
